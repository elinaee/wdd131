SitePlan

Site Name

Portugal - A Tourists Guide

I thought of this name, because it is short, catchy and sums up what the website is about - main places and foods to try when visiting Portugal.

Site Purpose

The site provides a list of recommended places to visit and foods and drinks to try when visiting Portugal's two biggest cities, along with the expected weather for both of them and a short info about Portugal.

Scenarios

  1. Foods
    • What are some of Portugal's traditional foods?
    • What are some of the most traditional pastries for some known towns in the Lisbon district?
    • What are the best months to eat snails in Portugal?
  2. Places to visit
    • What are some of the best castles that I should visit when in Lisbon?
    • What are some of the coolest activities to do whilst in Porto?
    • What are some of the best coastal towns to visit in Lisbon?
